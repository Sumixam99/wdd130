The Voices of the Book of Mormon

PURPOSE: The purpose of this website is to provide a new way to look at the Book of Mormon to its readers. By being able to easily see who is speaking at any moment in the Book of Mormon, readers will be able to better understand the speaker and hopefully connect with them. It will also provide a way to demonstrate the differences of style and personality of different voices from the Book of Mormon.

ABOUT: This website contains part of the Book of Mormon. In it, the text of the Book of Mormon is colored based on who is the current voice. This will help the reader identify who the current speaker is easily. This also provides an easy way to see everything an individual has said in the scriptures. This is similar to a red-letter edition of the Bible, which colors all of the words of Jesus in red making them easy to find.
